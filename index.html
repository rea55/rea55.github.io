How to use

Type your Starting Location into the Search bar. Press the "Choose As A" Button. The "A" Marker shows your starting Location. Now type your Ending Location and choose it as "B". The "B" Marker shows your End location. The Map will now Display up to 3 Roads. If there are less, that means that the other Routes are way too long
